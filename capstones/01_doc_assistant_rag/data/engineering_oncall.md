# Engineering On-Call Runbook

## P1 incidents

For **P1 incidents**, the primary on-call engineer must acknowledge within 5 minutes.

Current rotation is published in PagerDuty. Escalation path: on-call → engineering manager → CTO.

## P2 incidents

Response within 30 minutes during business hours.

## Handoff

End-of-week handoff requires a written summary in the team wiki.
