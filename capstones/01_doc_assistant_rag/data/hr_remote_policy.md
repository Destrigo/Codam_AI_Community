# Remote Work Policy

**Effective:** 2024-01-01  
**Owner:** HR

## Eligibility

All full-time employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** after completing probation.

## Requirements

- Core hours 10:00–16:00 in local timezone
- Must attend monthly in-person team day
- VPN required for internal systems

## Exceptions

Engineering on-call staff must be reachable within 15 minutes on on-call weeks regardless of location.
